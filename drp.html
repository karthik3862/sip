Majhi Kanya Bhagyashree

Scheme Details

The Majhi Kanya Bhagyashree scheme is an initiative by the Maharashtra Government to improve the status of girl children in the state. The scheme aims to discourage gender discrimination, promote education, and provide financial security for the future of the girl child.

Benefits

  • Financial assistance provided to families of girl children.
  • Initial deposit of ₹5,000 in a fixed deposit for each girl child.
  • Additional financial benefits as the girl child progresses in education.
  • Promotes gender equality and empowerment of girl children.
  • Support for healthcare and nutrition needs of the girl child.

Eligibility

  • Families with up to two girl children are eligible.
  • The scheme is applicable for families residing in Maharashtra.
  • Annual family income should not exceed ₹7.5 lakh.
  • Parents must provide proof of girl child registration and immunization records.

Application Process

  • Visit the nearest Anganwadi center or government health center.
  • Fill out the application form for the scheme.
  • Provide necessary documents for verification, including proof of residence and income.
  • Submit birth certificate and immunization records of the girl child.
  • Upon verification, financial benefits will be disbursed to the family.

Documents Required

  • Proof of identity (Aadhaar card, Voter ID, etc.).
  • Birth certificate of the girl child.
  • Proof of residence (e.g., utility bill, ration card, etc.).
  • Income certificate of the family.
  • Immunization records of the girl child.
