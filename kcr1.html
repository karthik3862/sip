KCR Nutrition Kit

Scheme Details

The KCR Nutrition Kit is a special initiative by the Telangana government to improve maternal and child health. The kit contains essential nutritional supplements for pregnant women, ensuring they receive the necessary nutrients during pregnancy, which contributes to the health of both mother and child.

Benefits

  • Improved nutrition for pregnant women to ensure healthy pregnancies.
  • Reduction in maternal and infant mortality rates.
  • Enhanced awareness about prenatal health and nutrition.
  • Access to essential nutrients like iron, calcium, and vitamins.
  • Support for families from economically disadvantaged backgrounds.

Eligibility

  • Pregnant women who are residents of Telangana.
  • Women registered with government healthcare centers.
  • Priority given to women from economically weaker sections.

Application Process

  • Visit the nearest government healthcare facility or Anganwadi center.
  • Register for the scheme and provide necessary information.
  • Undergo a health check-up to confirm eligibility.
  • Receive the KCR Nutrition Kit as part of routine prenatal visits.
  • Follow guidelines provided by healthcare professionals for using the kit.

Documents Required

  • Proof of identity (Aadhaar card, Voter ID, etc.).
  • Proof of residence (e.g., utility bill, ration card, etc.).
  • Pregnancy confirmation certificate from a registered medical practitioner.
  • Income certificate (if applicable).
