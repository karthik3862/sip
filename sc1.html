Senior Citizens Health Insurance Scheme

Scheme Details

The Senior Citizens Health Insurance Scheme aims to provide affordable health insurance coverage to senior citizens, ensuring access to healthcare services without financial strain. The scheme is designed to cover medical expenses, hospitalization, and critical illness treatments for senior citizens, improving their quality of life in their later years.

Benefits

  • Coverage for hospitalization expenses, including room charges, medical procedures, and doctor’s fees.
  • Pre- and post-hospitalization coverage for medical tests and consultations.
  • Critical illness coverage for senior citizens diagnosed with life-threatening diseases like cancer, heart disease, etc.
  • Cashless treatment at network hospitals under the scheme.
  • Annual health check-ups and preventive healthcare services.

Eligibility

  • Must be a senior citizen aged 60 years or above.
  • Must be an Indian resident.
  • Should not have any pre-existing medical conditions that are not covered under the scheme.
  • Must be enrolled under the scheme by providing the necessary documents and paying the premium (if applicable).

Application Process

  • Visit the nearest authorized insurance office or agent.
  • Provide proof of age (Aadhaar card, Voter ID, etc.) and medical history.
  • Fill out the health insurance application form and submit the required documents.
  • Undergo a medical check-up, if required, as per the scheme’s guidelines.
  • Pay the applicable premium (if any) and wait for approval and policy issuance.

Documents Required

  • Proof of age (Aadhaar card, Voter ID, birth certificate).
  • Recent passport-size photograph.
  • Medical records (if any pre-existing conditions or treatments).
  • Proof of residence (address proof).
  • Bank account details for policy-related payments (if required).
