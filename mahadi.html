Maharashtra Diabetes Insurance Scheme

Scheme Details

The Maharashtra Diabetes Insurance Scheme provides financial protection to individuals diagnosed with diabetes, covering medical expenses related to diabetes management, such as hospitalization, medication, and diagnostics. The scheme aims to improve the accessibility of healthcare for diabetes patients and reduce the financial burden of long-term diabetes care.

Benefits

  • Insurance coverage for medical expenses related to diabetes management, including hospitalization, medication, and diagnostics.
  • Coverage for complications arising from diabetes, such as heart disease, kidney disease, and eye problems.
  • Cashless treatment in network hospitals for diabetes-related health services.
  • Access to diabetes care specialists and regular health check-ups.
  • Prevention programs for diabetes-related complications, including awareness campaigns and health education.
  • Support for diabetes patients in need of insulin, blood glucose testing strips, and other essential medical supplies.

Eligibility

  • Residents of Maharashtra who have been diagnosed with diabetes.
  • Individuals who are below the income ceiling set by the state government (for subsidized premiums).
  • Diabetes patients with a valid health insurance claim in need of diabetes-related treatment.

Application Process

  • Visit the nearest government health facility or insurance office to apply for the scheme.
  • Submit medical records or diagnostic reports confirming the diagnosis of diabetes.
  • Fill out the application form and choose your preferred insurance plan.
  • Complete the documentation and submit necessary identity and income proof (if required).
  • Once approved, you will receive an insurance policy for diabetes care and treatment.

Documents Required

  • Proof of identity (Aadhaar card, Voter ID, etc.).
  • Proof of diagnosis (diabetes report, doctor's certificate, etc.).
  • Income proof (for subsidized premium eligibility).
  • Passport-size photograph.
  • Bank details for policy premium payment (if applicable).
