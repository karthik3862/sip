Janani Suraksha Yojana (JSY)

Scheme Details

Janani Suraksha Yojana (JSY) is a safe motherhood intervention under the National Rural Health Mission (NRHM) aimed at reducing maternal and neonatal mortality by promoting institutional delivery among pregnant women. The scheme provides financial assistance to pregnant women for delivering their babies in health institutions.

Benefits

  • Cash assistance for pregnant women to encourage institutional deliveries.
  • Financial assistance for transportation to and from healthcare facilities.
  • Improvement in maternal and neonatal health outcomes.
  • Encouragement of safe childbirth and postnatal care for mother and child.
  • Targeted towards rural, backward, and BPL (Below Poverty Line) women.

Eligibility

  • Pregnant women from Below Poverty Line (BPL) families.
  • Women who deliver in government-recognized health institutions.
  • Women who have at least one institutional delivery (not applicable to women who are delivering in private institutions).
  • Women who are aged 19 years or above.
  • Women who are not availing benefits of other maternity schemes.

Application Process

  • Visit the nearest government health facility or Anganwadi center.
  • Fill out the JSY application form with required personal details and proof of pregnancy.
  • Submit documents and undergo a medical check-up as part of the registration process.
  • Undergo delivery in an approved government health institution (primary health center, community health center, or district hospital).
  • Receive cash assistance after delivery as per the norms of the scheme.
