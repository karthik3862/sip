Telangana Kidney Transplant Support Program

Scheme Details

The Telangana Kidney Transplant Support Program provides financial assistance to patients with end-stage kidney disease who need a kidney transplant. The program aims to make kidney transplants accessible to those in need, especially from Below Poverty Line (BPL) families across Telangana.

Benefits

  • Financial support for kidney transplant surgeries, including pre-surgery and post-surgery care.
  • Coverage of hospitalization costs related to the transplant procedure.
  • Medicines and diagnostic support for patients undergoing treatment.
  • Support for Below Poverty Line (BPL) families and those in need of financial assistance.

Eligibility

  • Resident of Telangana state.
  • Diagnosed with chronic kidney disease requiring a transplant.
  • Low-income or Below Poverty Line (BPL) families with valid documentation.
  • Patients must be registered with a government or empaneled hospital for kidney treatment.

Application Process

  • Visit the nearest government hospital or healthcare facility offering kidney transplant services.
  • Submit a medical report confirming kidney failure and the need for a transplant.
  • Provide required documents such as BPL certificate, income certificate, etc.
  • Complete the application form with hospital staff assistance. Upon approval, the support will be provided directly to the patient.

Documents Required

  • Proof of residence (Aadhaar card, Voter ID, or Ration card).
  • Income certificate or BPL card (if applicable).
  • Medical diagnosis report from a recognized hospital confirming the need for a kidney transplant.
  • Recent passport-sized photograph.
  • Proof of enrollment in a government health scheme (if applicable).
