National Kidney Transplant Scheme

Scheme Details

The National Kidney Transplant Scheme is a government initiative aimed at providing financial assistance for kidney transplant surgeries to patients who cannot afford the cost. The scheme ensures timely and quality treatment for patients in need of kidney transplants, especially from underprivileged backgrounds.

Benefits

  • Financial assistance for kidney transplant surgeries.
  • Free post-transplant care for eligible patients.
  • Assistance with organ transplantation across India.
  • Subsidized medicines and diagnostic services post-transplant.
  • Access to government and empaneled private hospitals for transplant surgeries.

Eligibility

  • Must be a citizen of India.
  • Must be diagnosed with end-stage renal disease requiring a kidney transplant.
  • Patients must have financial limitations and be from economically weaker sections.
  • Patients must be registered with authorized hospitals and have a recommendation for transplant from a medical professional.

Application Process

  • Visit the nearest government hospital or any empaneled private hospital.
  • Submit the necessary medical documents and diagnosis report.
  • Fill out the application form for the scheme, available at the hospital.
  • Once the application is processed, eligibility will be verified, and financial assistance will be provided for the transplant surgery.

Documents Required

  • Proof of residence (Aadhaar card, Voter ID, or Ration card).
  • Income certificate (if applicable).
  • Medical diagnosis report from a registered hospital.
  • Medical recommendation for a kidney transplant from a certified doctor.
  • Recent passport-sized photograph.
