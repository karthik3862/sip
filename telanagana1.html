Telangana Kidney Health Assistance Scheme

Scheme Details

The Telangana Kidney Health Assistance Scheme is aimed at providing comprehensive support for individuals affected by kidney diseases. The scheme offers free dialysis, kidney transplant surgeries, and post-transplant care to the economically disadvantaged residents of Telangana.

Benefits

  • Free dialysis sessions at government hospitals and empaneled private centers.
  • Financial support for kidney transplant surgeries.
  • Subsidized immunosuppressive drugs post-transplant.
  • Nephrology care and treatment for Below Poverty Line (BPL) families.

Eligibility

  • Resident of Telangana.
  • Diagnosed with chronic kidney disease or renal failure.
  • Belonging to BPL families, with a valid ration card or white card.
  • Patients must be registered at government or empaneled hospitals.

Application Process

  • Visit the nearest government hospital or Health Centre.
  • Submit medical diagnosis reports and the necessary documents.
  • Fill out the application form with the assistance of hospital staff.
  • Verification and approval will be managed by the district health department.

Documents Required

  • Proof of residence (Aadhaar card, Voter ID, or Ration card).
  • Income certificate or BPL white card.
  • Medical diagnosis report certified by a nephrologist.
