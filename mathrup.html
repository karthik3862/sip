Arogya Lakshmi Scheme

Scheme Details

The Arogya Lakshmi scheme, initiated by the Telangana government, provides one full meal to pregnant and lactating women at Anganwadi centers. The scheme aims to improve the health of mothers and their newborns by addressing malnutrition and ensuring adequate nutritional intake.

Benefits

  • One full meal served daily to pregnant and lactating women at Anganwadi centers.
  • Meals include rice, dal with leafy vegetables, eggs, and milk to ensure balanced nutrition.
  • Improves maternal and child health by reducing malnutrition and anemia.
  • Encourages women to regularly visit Anganwadi centers for health check-ups.
  • Free distribution of iron and folic acid tablets to women under the scheme.

Eligibility

  • Pregnant and lactating women residing in Telangana.
  • Women registered at the nearest Anganwadi center.
  • Primarily aimed at women from Below Poverty Line (BPL) families.
  • Women up to six months post-delivery are eligible for the scheme.

Application Process

  • Visit the nearest Anganwadi center to enroll in the scheme.
  • Submit proof of pregnancy or lactation status (medical certificate).
  • Provide necessary identification and proof of residence.
  • Regularly attend Anganwadi centers to avail meal services and health check-ups.

Documents Required

  • Proof of identity (Aadhaar card, Voter ID, etc.).
  • Medical certificate confirming pregnancy or lactation.
  • Proof of residence (e.g., utility bill, ration card, etc.).
  • BPL certificate (if applicable).
  • Photographs for registration.
