Maharashtra Kidney Transplant Support Scheme

Scheme Details

The Maharashtra Kidney Transplant Support Scheme aims to provide financial assistance and support for kidney transplants to the residents of Maharashtra who are suffering from end-stage kidney disease. The scheme helps patients in need of kidney transplants by covering part of the medical expenses, including transplant surgery, dialysis, and post-transplant care.

Benefits

  • Financial assistance for kidney transplant surgeries and related procedures.
  • Coverage for pre-transplant tests and post-transplant care.
  • Support for dialysis treatment for patients awaiting kidney transplants.
  • Helps in covering the cost of medicines and hospitalization during the treatment process.
  • Priority treatment for low-income patients and those with financial difficulties.

Eligibility

  • Must be a resident of Maharashtra.
  • Must be suffering from end-stage renal disease (ESRD) requiring a kidney transplant.
  • Must be economically disadvantaged and unable to afford the transplant cost.
  • Must be registered with a participating hospital for transplant services.
  • Must provide necessary medical documents and test results indicating the need for a transplant.

Application Process

  • Visit the nearest government hospital or any participating healthcare center.
  • Fill out the application form for the Maharashtra Kidney Transplant Support Scheme.
  • Submit necessary medical documents, including kidney function tests and a transplant recommendation from a certified doctor.
  • Provide proof of residency and income certificate (if applicable) to qualify for financial assistance.
  • Once approved, the hospital will schedule the transplant surgery and assist with the pre- and post-surgery care.

Documents Required

  • Proof of identity (Aadhaar card, Voter ID, Ration card, etc.).
  • Proof of residency in Maharashtra (e.g., utility bills, ration card).
  • Medical reports including kidney function tests, diagnosis of end-stage renal disease.
  • Income certificate (if applicable) to avail financial assistance.
  • Passport-sized photograph of the applicant.
