Karnataka Kidney Transplant Support Program

Scheme Details

The Karnataka Kidney Transplant Support Program is designed to assist kidney patients in the state who require transplant surgeries. The program covers transplant-related costs, including pre-operative and post-operative expenses, for patients from low-income families.

Benefits

  • Coverage for kidney transplant surgeries, including pre-surgery tests and post-surgery medications.
  • Financial assistance for hospitalization and follow-up treatment.
  • Support for Below Poverty Line (BPL) families and those unable to afford the procedure.
  • Access to a network of empaneled hospitals for kidney transplantation.

Eligibility

  • Resident of Karnataka.
  • Diagnosed with end-stage kidney failure requiring a transplant.
  • Low-income or BPL families with the required documentation.
  • Patients must be registered with a government hospital or empaneled healthcare provider for treatment.

Application Process

  • Visit the nearest government hospital or healthcare facility offering kidney transplant services.
  • Provide a valid medical report confirming kidney failure and the need for transplant surgery.
  • Submit required documents such as BPL certificate and income proof.
  • Fill out the application form and submit it for approval. Once approved, the support will be provided.

Documents Required

  • Proof of residence (Aadhaar card, Voter ID, or Ration card).
  • Income certificate or BPL card (if applicable).
  • Medical diagnosis report from a recognized healthcare provider confirming the need for a kidney transplant.
  • Recent passport-sized photograph.
  • Proof of enrollment in a government health scheme (if applicable).
