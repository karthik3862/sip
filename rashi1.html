Rashtriya Vayoshri Yojana (RVSY) - Medical Support

Scheme Details

The Rashtriya Vayoshri Yojana (RVSY) is a government initiative aimed at providing medical support to senior citizens who are living below the poverty line (BPL) in India. The scheme focuses on supplying assistive devices like hearing aids, walking aids, and spectacles to elderly individuals to improve their quality of life.

Benefits

  • Provision of free assistive devices such as hearing aids, walking sticks, wheelchairs, and spectacles.
  • Improves mobility and communication for senior citizens with disabilities.
  • Enhances the quality of life for elderly individuals in BPL families.
  • Medical assistance for the elderly population to help them live a better life with dignity.

Eligibility

  • Indian citizens aged 60 years or above.
  • Senior citizens who are living Below Poverty Line (BPL).
  • Those who require assistive devices to enhance mobility and improve quality of life.
  • Individuals with specific medical needs related to visual, hearing, or mobility impairments.

Application Process

  • Visit the nearest government hospital or Primary Health Centre (PHC) that is implementing the Rashtriya Vayoshri Yojana.
  • Fill out the application form available at the center.
  • Submit required documents, including proof of age and income status (BPL certificate).
  • Once your application is approved, you will be provided with the required assistive devices.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar card or any government-issued ID card for identity verification.
  • Proof of age (e.g., Birth certificate, Aadhaar card, or other valid ID).
  • Below Poverty Line (BPL) certificate or relevant income proof.
  • Medical prescriptions or doctor's recommendation for the required assistive devices (if applicable).
  • Recent passport-sized photograph.
