Free Medical Camps for Senior Citizens

Scheme Details

The Free Medical Camps for Senior Citizens program provides free medical check-ups, consultations, and treatment for elderly citizens. These camps aim to address the healthcare needs of senior citizens, particularly those from low-income backgrounds who may have limited access to medical facilities.

Benefits

  • Free health check-ups and consultations with doctors.
  • Free medication and treatment for common age-related health issues.
  • Free diagnostic tests such as blood pressure, diabetes, and cholesterol screenings.
  • Awareness programs on healthy living and common health issues in senior citizens.
  • Referral to specialized medical care if needed.

Eligibility

  • Must be a senior citizen (typically 60 years and above).
  • Open to all senior citizens, regardless of income or background.
  • Must reside in the local area where the medical camps are being held.
  • Participants may be required to bring proof of age (e.g., Aadhaar card, voter ID).

Application Process

  • Visit the location where the medical camp is being held (refer to participating locations below).
  • No prior registration is required; senior citizens can walk in directly during the camp hours.
  • Complete a simple registration form (if required) to document participation.
  • Receive a health check-up and necessary medical advice.
  • Follow up with any referrals or prescriptions as needed.

Documents Required

  • Proof of identity (Aadhaar card, Voter ID, etc.).
  • Proof of age (Birth certificate, Aadhaar card, etc.).
  • Any prior medical records (if available) to help doctors understand the patient's health condition.
  • Prescription for any ongoing medications (if applicable).
