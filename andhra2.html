Andhra Pradesh Kidney Transplant Assistance Scheme

Scheme Details

The Andhra Pradesh Kidney Transplant Assistance Scheme provides financial support for patients requiring kidney transplants. This initiative is aimed at easing the burden of medical expenses for economically weaker families in the state.

Benefits

  • Financial aid for kidney transplant surgeries.
  • Coverage for post-operative care, including immunosuppressive medications.
  • Free diagnostic tests and specialized nephrology consultations.
  • Priority care for Below Poverty Line (BPL) families.

Eligibility

  • Resident of Andhra Pradesh.
  • Diagnosed with end-stage renal disease (ESRD) requiring a transplant.
  • BPL families with valid ration or white card.
  • Transplant recommended by a government-recognized nephrologist.

Application Process

  • Visit the nearest government hospital or authorized transplant center.
  • Submit medical reports and required documents for verification.
  • Fill out the application form with assistance from the hospital staff.
  • The application will be reviewed, and approval will be granted based on eligibility.

Documents Required

  • Proof of residence (Aadhaar card, Voter ID, or Ration card).
  • Income certificate or BPL white card.
  • Medical reports certified by a nephrologist.
  • Recommendation letter for transplant surgery.
  • Photographs of the patient and donor (if applicable).
