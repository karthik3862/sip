Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana

Scheme Details

The Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PMJAY), also known as Ayushman Bharat, is a flagship healthcare scheme launched by the Government of India. It aims to provide health insurance coverage to economically vulnerable families, ensuring access to quality healthcare services without financial burden. Under PMJAY, eligible families can avail free treatment for hospitalization, surgeries, and medical procedures in empaneled hospitals across India.

Benefits

  • Free health insurance coverage for up to Rs. 5 lakh per family per year for hospitalization expenses.
  • Covers medical treatment, surgery, and diagnostics in both public and private hospitals.
  • Pre- and post-hospitalization expenses are also covered under the scheme.
  • Includes treatment for major illnesses such as heart disease, cancer, diabetes, and other chronic diseases.
  • Empaneled private hospitals provide medical treatment to beneficiaries under the scheme.
  • Cashless and paperless services at participating hospitals.

Eligibility

  • Must belong to an economically vulnerable family as identified by the Socio-Economic Caste Census (SECC) 2011 data.
  • Families in rural areas under the “deprived” category (landless laborers, manual scavengers, etc.) are eligible.
  • Families in urban areas under the “occupational” category (such as rag pickers, construction workers, etc.) are eligible.
  • Citizens of India, living in eligible areas, can apply for the scheme.
  • Persons with pre-existing health conditions are also covered under the scheme.

Application Process

  • Visit the nearest Common Service Center (CSC) or participating hospital to check eligibility.
  • Fill out the application form with required details such as family members, income details, and residence proof.
  • Submit documents like Aadhaar card, income certificate, ration card, and proof of residence.
  • Once your details are verified, you will receive an Ayushman Bharat card that grants access to the benefits.
  • Visit any empaneled hospital for treatment under the PMJAY scheme.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar card (for identification).
  • Income certificate (if applicable) to prove eligibility under the scheme.
  • Ration card or other proof of residence (e.g., utility bills, voter ID).
  • Photograph of the applicant (passport-sized).
  • Bank account details (for reimbursement purposes, if needed).
