Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY)

Scheme Details

Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY) is a maternity benefit program launched by the Government of India. It provides financial assistance to pregnant and lactating women to support them during their pregnancy and the early months after childbirth. The scheme aims to provide conditional cash transfers to improve the health and nutrition of the mother and the newborn.

Benefits

  • Cash incentive of Rs. 5,000 during pregnancy and childbirth.
  • Improvement in maternal health and nutrition.
  • Improved birth outcomes by promoting institutional deliveries.
  • Encouragement of better maternal care and vaccination for the child.
  • Financial support to reduce the economic burden during pregnancy.

Eligibility

  • Pregnant women and lactating mothers (up to the first child).
  • Women above 19 years of age.
  • First-time mothers who are not covered under any other government scheme.
  • Women who do not receive maternity benefits from the employer.

Application Process

  • Visit the nearest Anganwadi center or Health Sub-center.
  • Fill out the application form for PMMVY.
  • Provide necessary documents for verification (proof of pregnancy, identity, etc.).
  • Undergo necessary health check-ups and register for the scheme.
  • Receive cash transfer directly into the beneficiary’s bank account in installments.

Documents Required

  • Proof of identity (Aadhaar card, Voter ID, etc.).
  • Proof of pregnancy (Medical certificate or doctor’s note).
  • Bank account details (for direct cash transfer).
  • Proof of age (birth certificate or other official documents).
