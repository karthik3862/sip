Andhra Pradesh Kidney Care Scheme

Scheme Details

The Andhra Pradesh Kidney Care Scheme offers medical and financial support to individuals suffering from chronic kidney diseases. The scheme ensures affordable treatment, including dialysis, transplants, and post-operative care, for economically weaker sections.

Benefits

  • Free dialysis sessions at government hospitals and empaneled private centers.
  • Financial aid for kidney transplant surgeries.
  • Subsidized immunosuppressive drugs post-transplant.
  • Specialized nephrology care for BPL families.

Eligibility

  • Resident of Andhra Pradesh.
  • Diagnosed with chronic kidney disease or renal failure.
  • BPL families with valid ration card or white card.
  • Patients registered at government or empaneled hospitals.

Application Process

  • Visit the nearest government hospital or Health Centre.
  • Provide medical diagnosis reports and required documents.
  • Fill out the application form with assistance from hospital staff.
  • Verification and approval will be handled by the district health department.

Documents Required

  • Proof of residence (Aadhaar card, Voter ID, or Ration card).
  • Income certificate or BPL white card.
  • Medical diagnosis report certified by a nephrologist.
