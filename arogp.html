Dr. Muthulakshmi Reddy Maternity Benefit Scheme

Scheme Details

The Dr. Muthulakshmi Reddy Maternity Benefit Scheme is an initiative by the Tamil Nadu government to provide financial assistance to pregnant women to promote safe motherhood and reduce maternal and infant mortality rates. It offers cash benefits in installments to help meet nutritional and medical expenses during pregnancy.

Benefits

  • Financial assistance of up to â‚¹18,000 for pregnant women.
  • Promotes institutional deliveries to ensure maternal and child safety.
  • Encourages antenatal care and regular health check-ups during pregnancy.
  • Supports nutritional needs and reduces economic strain on families.
  • Prevention and treatment of anemia among pregnant women.

Eligibility

  • Pregnant women residing in Tamil Nadu.
  • Women from Below Poverty Line (BPL) families are prioritized.
  • First two live births are covered under the scheme.
  • Applicants must register at the nearest health facility during the first trimester.

Application Process

  • Visit the nearest government health facility or Primary Health Centre (PHC).
  • Submit proof of pregnancy from a certified healthcare provider.
  • Provide necessary identification and residence proof documents.
  • Complete the registration process for regular antenatal care check-ups.

Documents Required

  • Proof of identity (Aadhaar card, Voter ID, etc.).
  • Medical certificate confirming pregnancy.
  • Proof of residence (e.g., utility bill, ration card, etc.).
  • BPL certificate (if applicable).
  • Bank account details for direct transfer of benefits.
