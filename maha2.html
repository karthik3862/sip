Maharashtra Kidney Health Assistance Scheme

Scheme Details

The Maharashtra Kidney Health Assistance Scheme provides financial assistance to individuals suffering from kidney-related diseases, including dialysis and kidney transplant treatments. The scheme aims to support residents of Maharashtra by covering medical expenses and facilitating access to necessary treatments for those unable to afford them.

Benefits

  • Financial assistance for dialysis and kidney transplant surgery.
  • Support for pre-treatment tests and post-treatment care.
  • Help in covering the cost of essential medications and hospitalization during treatment.
  • Priority treatment for low-income individuals and families.
  • Availability of medical assistance for kidney disease prevention and management.

Eligibility

  • Must be a resident of Maharashtra.
  • Must be diagnosed with chronic kidney disease or require dialysis or a kidney transplant.
  • Must meet the income criteria set by the Maharashtra government to qualify for financial assistance.
  • Must be registered with a participating hospital for treatment.
  • Should submit the necessary medical documentation supporting the need for treatment.

Application Process

  • Visit a participating hospital or government healthcare center in Maharashtra.
  • Fill out the application form for the Maharashtra Kidney Health Assistance Scheme.
  • Submit required medical reports, including a diagnosis of kidney disease and a treatment recommendation from a qualified doctor.
  • Provide proof of residency in Maharashtra and income details (if applicable).
  • After approval, the hospital will coordinate with the patient for further medical procedures.

Documents Required

  • Proof of identity (Aadhaar card, Voter ID, etc.).
  • Proof of residency in Maharashtra (e.g., utility bills, ration card, etc.).
  • Medical reports that confirm the kidney-related illness or need for dialysis/ transplant.
  • Income certificate (if applicable) to qualify for financial assistance.
  • Passport-sized photographs of the applicant.
