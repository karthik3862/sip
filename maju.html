Rajshree Yojana

Scheme Details

Rajshree Yojana, launched by the Government of Rajasthan, is designed to improve the socio-economic status of girls in the state by providing financial support at various stages of their education and life. The scheme aims to combat gender discrimination and encourage education among girls.

Benefits

  • Financial assistance provided in installments from birth until the completion of education.
  • A total financial aid of up to â‚¹50,000 is provided under the scheme.
  • Encourages the enrollment of girls in schools and reduces dropout rates.
  • Improves the health, education, and well-being of girl children.
  • Promotes gender equality and empowerment of women.

Eligibility

  • Families residing in Rajasthan with a girl child born on or after 1st June 2016.
  • The girl child must be enrolled in school and have regular attendance.
  • Applicable for the first two girl children in a family.
  • Parents must register the birth of the girl child and obtain a birth certificate.

Application Process

  • Visit the nearest Anganwadi center or government health center in Rajasthan.
  • Fill out the application form for Rajshree Yojana.
  • Submit the necessary documents, including birth certificate and proof of school enrollment.
  • Verification will be conducted by the concerned authorities.
  • Upon approval, financial benefits will be disbursed in stages as per the scheme's provisions.

Documents Required

  • Proof of identity (Aadhaar card, Voter ID, etc.).
  • Birth certificate of the girl child.
  • Proof of residence (e.g., utility bill, ration card, etc.).
  • Proof of school enrollment and attendance.
  • Bank account details of the beneficiary or guardian.
