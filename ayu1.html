Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PMJAY)

Scheme Details

Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PMJAY) is a government-backed health insurance scheme launched by the Government of India. The scheme aims to provide free medical treatment to economically disadvantaged families by covering hospitalization expenses for secondary and tertiary care treatments.

Benefits

  • Health insurance coverage of up to â‚¹5 lakh per family per year.
  • Covers hospitalization expenses, including pre and post-hospitalization costs.
  • Covers a wide range of medical treatments including surgeries, maternity care, and critical care.
  • Cashless and paperless access to health services across empaneled hospitals.
  • No restrictions on family size, age, or gender.

Eligibility

  • Targeted towards Below Poverty Line (BPL) families, as identified by the Socio-Economic Caste Census (SECC).
  • People from vulnerable social groups including scheduled castes, scheduled tribes, and other marginalized groups.
  • Families who do not have any other form of health insurance or financial assistance for healthcare.
  • Indian citizens who are part of the eligible family list as per the scheme's guidelines.

Application Process

  • Visit the nearest empaneled hospital or health facility offering PMJAY services.
  • Check your eligibility through the PMJAY website or by visiting the hospital.
  • Provide the necessary details such as Aadhaar number and family information for enrollment.
  • If eligible, you will be issued a PMJAY card, which provides access to medical benefits.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card for identification.
  • Proof of family income (if applicable).
  • Photograph of the family head or applicant.
  • Ration card or SECC family data to verify eligibility.
  • Any government-issued ID for verification of identity.
